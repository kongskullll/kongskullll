PAPER PRESENTATIONS

Paper presentation is an oral presentation where participants required to make a paper on emerging technologies and present it infront of juries. Each paper presentation will be a maximum of 12 minutes in length and will be followed by a brief question and answer period.
POSTER PRESENTATION

A poster itself is a visual presentation comprising whatever the contributor wishes to display on the poster board. The purpose of a poster is to outline a piece of work in a form that is easily assimilated and stimulates interest and discussion.
CODE HUNT

Code Hunt is unique as an online coding platform in that each puzzle is presented with test cases only, no specification. Players have to first work out the pattern and then code the answer.
TECHNICAL QUIZ

This quiz competition is a pursuit of technical knowledge and actually tests the students retention and accumulation of knowledge towards application of concepts. It would be greatly helpful in acknowledging their position on their journey to technical excellence.
TYPING

Typing is the process of writing or inputting text by pressing keys on a computer keyboard. Text can be in the form of letters, numbers and other symbols.
WEB DESIGNING

Web designers plan, create and code internet sites and web pages, many of which combine text with sounds, pictures, graphics and video clips.It and can mean working on a brand new website or updating an already existing site.
